Transparency prototype

Showing how readiness support is distributed.

This page is an early prototype of how TASK L3C will communicate the movement of support across disaster phases, regions, and partners in Japan.

Concept

How transparency will work

TASK L3C is designed to make it easy to see where readiness support is going and how it strengthens Japan’s disaster cycle. Future iterations will display real data drawn from digital records of support flows.
