Programs

Readiness support that protects primary missions.

TASK L3C strengthens disaster readiness in Japan before the next event. We do this by funding and connecting trusted frontline NGOs and NPOs without pulling them away from their everyday missions.

Three pillars

How TASK operates

Pre‑disaster funding

Stable readiness resources positioned early, so partners don’t start from zero when disaster hits.

Capacity building

Planning, training, and tools built with local expertise to increase speed, safety, and coordination.

Bridging & mutual support

Mutual‑aid roles mapped across NGOs/NPOs and global supporters connected before the event.
